## One Plan: Delivery Partner Income Shield

Aegis enrollment is structured for gig workers:

- Enrollment window: 2 months
- Premium cadence: weekly
- Premium collection: UPI AutoPay (e-mandate), independent of the delivery platform

## Weekly Premium Identity (Affordability-first)

Aegis models weekly premium using a compact actuarial identity:

`Pr_final = (Ew * alpha) * Lf * Ct * (1 + M)`

Where:

- `Ew`: rolling 4-week average earnings baseline
- `alpha`: affordable risk fraction (microinsurance benchmark), default `0.015`
- `Lf`: composite loss fraction for the rider's H3 zone and current week
- `Ct`: coverage tier factor (Basic/Standard/Premium)
- `M`: sustainability margin for risk pool stability

### Composite Loss Fraction

`Lf = 1 - Π(i=1..n) [ 1 - Pi * Si ]`

Aegis treats disruption probabilities and severities as a *composite trigger*, not independent events—so correlated risks (e.g., rain + waterlogging) are handled at the model level.

## Tier Coverage

| Tier | Covered Events | Rider segment |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | severe weather events (rain, flood, extreme heat) | essential protection |
| Standard | Basic + AQI events | full-time metro workers |
| Premium | Standard + government-declared disruptions | high-risk zones / high-earnings weeks |

### Coverage Matrix (tier mapping)

| Disruption | Category | Basic | Standard | Premium |
|---|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Heavy Rain (>60mm) | Environmental | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Flood / Zone inundation | Environmental | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Extreme Heat / Heatwave | Environmental | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Hazardous AQI (>300) | Environmental | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Severe AQI (200–300) | Environmental | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Bandh / Strike | Social | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Curfew / Local protest | Social | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Platform-declared zone halt | Social | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

## Premium Guardrails (Floor + Ceiling)

UPI AutoPay uses an enforcement cap:

- ceiling: `INR 150/week`
- floor: `INR 15/week`

To avoid unfairly charging part-timers the flat floor, Aegis uses **dynamic coverage scaling**:

If raw premium is below the floor, `Ct` increases so the rider still pays `INR 15/week` but receives higher effective payout coverage.

## What Is Not Covered

- vehicle repair
- fuel costs
- device damage
- personal illness
- platform-side cancellation without a qualifying trigger event
